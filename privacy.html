1. Introduction

SanfeReels respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the SanfeReels website and related TikTok integration features.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when authorized by the user:

  • Basic TikTok account information, such as user identifier, display name, or profile-related data made available through TikTok APIs
  • Video publishing metadata such as titles, captions, hashtags, and publishing preferences
  • Technical information necessary to operate the service

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate authorized users
  • Enable TikTok content upload and publishing workflows
  • Provide requested app functionality
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. TikTok Data Access

SanfeReels only accesses TikTok data and permissions explicitly authorized by the user through TikTok’s official authorization flow.

Access is limited to the scopes approved by the user and granted through the TikTok for Developers platform.

5. Data Sharing

SanfeReels does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with service providers, infrastructure providers, or where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain functionality, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

8. User Rights

Users may request information about their data, request correction or deletion where applicable, and revoke TikTok authorization at any time through TikTok settings or by contacting us.

9. Children’s Privacy

SanfeReels is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
