About SanfeReels

SanfeReels helps authorized users prepare, upload, and manage TikTok video content through an external workflow using official TikTok developer tools.

The app is intended for creators or account owners who want to simplify their TikTok publishing workflow.

Features

  • TikTok login and authorization
  • Video upload workflow
  • Support for titles, captions, and hashtags
  • Draft and publishing workflow integration
  • Use of official TikTok developer APIs

Data Usage

SanfeReels only accesses TikTok account information and content publishing permissions that are explicitly authorized by the user.

The app does not sell personal data and only uses authorized information to enable TikTok content upload and publishing features.
